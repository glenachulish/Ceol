Ceòl – User Guide

      
Click a section to expand it.


      

        
Getting around

        
LibraryBrowse all your tunes. Search by title, filter by type, key, mode, rating, hitlist, or collection. Click any tune card to open it.

        
SetsGroup tunes into named practice sets. Reorder with ↑↓, play a Transition between tunes, or click Music to open a tune's sheet music.

        
CollectionsThemed groups of tunes and sets (e.g. "Scottish Session"). Export as a .ceol.json to share with other Ceòl users.

        
More menuNotes documents, Achievements timeline, and a mobile link to open Ceòl on your phone over Wi-Fi.

        
🎙 FolkFriendOpens FolkFriend.app — hum or play a tune into your mic to identify it, like TunePal.

        
Links menuQuick links to TheSession.org, FlutefFling.scot, Nigel Gatherer, Hands Up for Trad, Flute Geezers, and Michael Eskin's ABC Tools.

      


      

        
Adding tunes

        
TheSession.orgSearch the world's largest trad database, preview the sheet music, choose a setting, and save to your library.

        
FlutefFling.scotBrowse the FlutefFling catalogue and import tunes with one click — PDF and MP3 links included.

        
ABC FileUpload a .abc file to bulk-import many tunes at once.

        
Paste ABCPaste raw ABC notation directly. Each tune must start with X:.

        
FolderDrop a folder of .abc, MP3, and PDF files — Ceòl matches and imports everything together.

        
YouTubePaste a YouTube URL to create a tune entry with the video in notes. Title fetched automatically.

        
PDF / PhotosAttach PDF sheet music to existing tunes, or import photos of handwritten music (auto-enhanced).

        
Ceol fileImport a .ceol.json from another Ceòl library — tunes are merged, duplicates skipped, collection recreated.

        
Music ScannerImport a .msca file from the Music Scanner app — tune titles and ABC are extracted and added to your library.

      


      

        
Inside a tune

        
Sheet Music tabRenders ABC notation. For FlutefFling tunes without ABC, shows the original PDF instead.

        
Audio playbackPlay as MIDI (flute sound). Set a start and end bar to loop just that section.

        
🖊 HighlightToggle Highlight mode then click any bar to mark it amber for difficult passages. Saved permanently. Click ✕ Clear to remove all.

        
ABC Text tabEdit the raw ABC notation and Save & Re-render to update the sheet music.

        
Notes tabFree-text notes with auto-linked URLs — MP3s and YouTube play inline. Use 🎧 Attach audio or 📹 Attach video to upload media files.

        
Practice tabPhrase Builder: choose a bar range, rest bars, and tempo ramp (e.g. 60%→100% in 5% steps). Builds a looping practice sequence.

        
⭐ RatingRate 1–5 stars on the card or in the modal. Improvements are auto-logged in Achievements.

        
📌 HitlistFlag tunes you're working on. Filter to Hitlist-only from the Library toolbar.

        
VersionsGroup different settings of the same tune. Click a parent to see all versions.

      


      

        
Library & Collections

        
SearchAccent- and case-insensitive — searching "ceol" finds "Ceòl". Also searches alternate tune names.

        
FiltersFilter by type, key, mode, rating, hitlist, favourites, composer, or collection.

        
Select modeClick Select then tick 2+ tunes to group as versions or delete in bulk.

        
Export collectionCollections → ⬇ Export saves a .ceol.json with all tunes (ABC, notes, ratings) and sets.

        
Import collectionImport Tunes → Ceol file tab → drop in the .ceol.json → Merge into library.

      


      

        
Backup & management

        
💾 Export LibraryDownloads a ZIP of everything — tunes, sets, collections, notes, uploads. Do this before any major change.

        
📂 Import LibraryRestores from a ZIP backup. Replaces everything — backup first!

        
🔀 Merge LibraryMerges another library's tunes in without replacing existing data.

        
iCloud / DropboxStore your library in iCloud Drive or Dropbox so it syncs across Macs. Edit .env in the Ceol folder and set CEOL_DATA_DIR to your cloud folder path, then restart. To move an existing library, copy data/ceol.db and data/uploads/ to the new folder first. Don't run two instances pointing at the same folder simultaneously.
