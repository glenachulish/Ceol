Welcome to Ceòl

You've just signed up to Ceòl. This is your personal library of traditional music — tunes, sheet music, audio, recordings, sets.

The app runs on a Raspberry Pi sitting in my house. No-one else has access to your library, including me.

How to get around

The bottom of the screen has tabs: Library for your tunes, Sets, Collections for broader categories (eg. Diggers tunes), and Import for adding new tunes.

Your library comes with some tunes from my library to get you started.

Add your first tune

The easiest way to get tunes into Ceòl is to copy them from TheSession.org. There are different ways to do this. The quickest I've found is:

  • Open TheSession.org.
  • Search for the name of the tune you are after.
  • For the version of the tune that you like, click on the ABC notation option and copy that.
  • Click back on Ceòl and tap Import.
  • Tap the Paste ABC tab.
  • Paste what you copied into the big text box.
  • Tap Import.

Your new tune should appear in your library with sheet music and all the metadata filled in.

Hear it played

Tap any tune in your library to open it. You'll see the sheet music rendered properly. Tap play — Ceòl will play the tune through your device's speakers.

Mark your favourites

On every tune card in your library there are two little buttons in the corner:

  • Favourite
  • Hitlist — tunes you're working on.

You can also star-rate tunes 1–5. I use this to rate how well I know a tune.
