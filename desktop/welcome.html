⚠️ Security warning on first launch

Your Mac or Windows PC may show a warning saying this app is from an "unidentified developer" or asking if you're sure you want to open it. This is completely normal — it simply means the app hasn't been signed with an expensive developer certificate. Ceol is safe to use.

  • On Mac: right-click the app → "Open" → click "Open" again in the dialog.
  • On Windows: click "More info" → then "Run anyway".
✅ Your data stays on your computer. All your tunes, sets, and notes are saved in Documents/Ceol on your machine. Nothing is sent to the internet (except when you fetch tunes from TheSession.org, which you initiate yourself).
Still unsure? That's absolutely fine — contact Callum MacLellan and he'll be happy to walk you through it and confirm the app is safe.
